Full English Recipe

Description

This is what we call a hearty, full english breakfast.

This meal is a weekend meal, an absolute treat, had a rough night out? This is what you're having the next day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Bacon
  • Sausages
  • Baked Beans
  • Toast

Steps

  1. Turn on the cooker and heat up the pans
  2. Put some olive oil in the frying pan and put the sausages in
  3. After 10 minutes add the bacon to the same pan
  4. add the tomatoes & eggs in 5 minutes after the bacon
  5. Heat up the baked beans and put the toast in
  6. Voila
