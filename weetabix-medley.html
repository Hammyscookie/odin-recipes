weetabix-medley

Description

Good healthy kick start to the day, with the weetabix medley, slow release carbs and can get a protein version of the weetabix too which tatses the same!

Ingredients

  • Weetabix
  • Banana
  • Milk of your choosing
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Blueberries
  • Pumpkin Seeds

Step by step

  1. Put the milk on the weetabix
  2. chop the banan add it to the weetabix
  3. add the pumpin seeds and blueberries
  4. Finish off by covering the weetabix with cocoa powder
