Tuna Pasta

Description

This is a Tuna Pasta, high in protein , high in carbs, an absolute fueler

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Pasta
  • Creamy Pasta Sauce
  • Black Pepper

Step by Step

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Pre heat the pasta sauce
  3. Mash The Tuna into the sauce
  4. add it all together and add black pepper
